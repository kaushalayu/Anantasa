About Us

Anantasa Foundation is a community-centric nonprofit organisation based in New Delhi committed to uplifting underserved families through structured nutrition support and preventive healthcare initiatives. We build recurring social impact models that address persistent needs with dignity, sustainability, and measurable outcomes.
